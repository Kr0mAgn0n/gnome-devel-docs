A small set of pages containing essential design advice and rules.

Design Patterns

A larger collection of building blocks that you can use to design your application. The design patterns include a variety of different design elements that you can utilise. Some patterns describe individual user interface components, such as check boxes or lists. Others describe more complex arrangements of components.
